Bangladesh Launches New Satellite

Date: 05/01/2025

Time: 10.25 PM

Shahjahan Mahmood, chairman of the Bangladesh Satellite Company Ltd, has said that the deal for the second satellite will be finalized within two or three months and it will be launched in two or three years. The second satellite will be a Bangladesh Earth Observatory Satellite, he said. He was speaking at the sixth anniversary of the successful launching of the Bangabandhu-1, the country's first communication satellite at Dhaka Club on Sunday night..
